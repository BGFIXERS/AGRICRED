About AgriCred

AgriCred is a revolutionary platform that uses AI to analyze crop health, farm size, and productivity to assess creditworthiness for smallholder farmers. We use satellite imagery, drone footage, and smartphone photos to help farmers access microloans and improve their financial inclusion.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Crop Analysis

    Farmers can upload images, and our AI system assesses crop health and predicts yields.

  • Predictive Income Modeling

    Forecast income potential based on crop health, weather data, and market trends.

  • Instant Loan Offers

    Microloans are offered instantly to eligible farmers directly via their mobile wallet.

  • Embedded Advisory Services

    Our platform provides real-time advisory services to help improve yield and reduce risk.

How It Works

Step 1: Upload Crop Image

Farmers upload photos of their crops or receive automated scans via drones.

Step 2: AI Analysis

Our AI system analyzes crop type, health, and growth stage, providing an accurate assessment.

Step 3: Predict Yield and Credit Score

ML models predict harvest value, and a credit score is generated based on crop health.

Step 4: Instant Loan Approval

Eligible farmers are offered instant microloans via their mobile wallets for easy access.
